--- WHAT IS DIALSYNC ---

Section Title:
End-to-End Sales Call Intelligence

Description:
DialSync connects your agents' Android phones directly to an AI analytics pipeline. From the moment a call ends, the recording is uploaded, analyzed, and ready for review — no manual steps, no missed calls.

Three core pieces work together:

1. Android Agent App
   Installed on each sales agent's phone. Silently captures call logs and recordings in real-time. Works even when the phone is in battery-saver mode (FCM keeps it alive).

2. AI Analysis Pipeline
   n8n workflows + Google Gemini AI transcribe every recording and extract key insights — customer objections, questions raised, call outcome, agent behavior.

3. Web Dashboard
   A React-based admin portal where managers see all agents, all calls, full transcripts, health alerts, and performance trends — updated live.


--- FEATURES ---

Section Title:
Everything Your Sales Team Needs

Feature 1 — Real-Time Call Tracking
   Every call is logged the moment it ends. Status is automatically classified: Connected, Not Connected, or Pending. Nothing slips through.

Feature 2 — AI Transcripts & Analysis
   Google Gemini reads every recording. Get full transcripts, extracted objections, customer questions, and a summary — automatically, without human effort.

Feature 3 — Agent Health Monitoring
   Know instantly if an agent's app is working correctly. Battery optimization interference, missing permissions, high pending uploads — all surfaced as alerts before they cause data loss.

Feature 4 — OTA App Updates
   Push a new app version from the admin panel. Agents receive a silent update notification and install with one tap — no manual APK distribution.

Feature 5 — Historical Import
   Onboard new agents or recover missed data. Select any date range and the app will upload all past calls and recordings automatically.

Feature 6 — FCM Background Keep-Alive
   A Cloudflare cron sends a silent push notification every 30 minutes to wake the app — defeating OEM background killers on MIUI, ColorOS, and similar ROMs.

Feature 7 — Permanent Device Blocklist
   Removed or offboarded agents are blocked permanently at the server level. Their device ID can never re-register — data security guaranteed.
