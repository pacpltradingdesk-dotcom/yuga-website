--- WHAT IS IT ---

Section Title:
Your Daily Market Briefing, on Autopilot

Description:
Every trading day, two reports are generated and delivered to PACPL's WhatsApp groups automatically. The morning report sets up the day; the evening report closes it out. No holidays missed — the system knows the NSE calendar.

Reports are delivered to:
- PDF Groups (members receive the full report document)
- Image Channel (every page posted as a high-quality image for easy viewing)


--- REPORTS ---

Section Title:
Two Reports. Every Trading Day.

Morning Report — 8:45 AM IST
   A comprehensive pre-market briefing covering:
   • Global Indices — US, European, and Asian market performance overnight
   • FII / DII Flow — institutional money movement
   • Nifty & BankNifty — key levels, support, resistance
   • Stocks Radar — curated watchlist for the day

Evening Report — 4:00 PM IST
   A closing market summary covering:
   • Market Recap — what happened today and why
   • Top Gainers & Losers — best and worst performers
   • Sector Performance — which sectors led and lagged
   • Key Highlights — notable events and data points


--- HOW IT WORKS ---

Section Title:
Fully Automated Pipeline

Step 1 — Data Collection
   Real-time market data is pulled automatically from financial data sources — indices, FII/DII flows, individual stock data.

Step 2 — Report Generation
   Python scripts process the data and generate a professionally formatted multi-page PDF with charts, tables, and analysis.

Step 3 — Image Conversion
   The PDF is converted to high-resolution images — one per page — for WhatsApp channel delivery.

Step 4 — WhatsApp Delivery
   Reports are sent to configured groups and channels via automated browser session. Delivery is confirmed before the process ends.

Step 5 — Self-Monitoring
   If anything goes wrong — send failure, login expiry, data issue — an automatic alert is sent to the admin's personal WhatsApp number.


--- INFRASTRUCTURE ---

Section Title:
Running 24/7 on Cloud Infrastructure

Description:
The Market Report system is deployed on a dedicated VPS in Mumbai (India) — ensuring low latency for NSE data access and reliable 8:45 AM / 4:00 PM delivery every trading day, regardless of whether any local computer is on.

Infrastructure:
• Server: Hostinger KVM 2 — 2 vCPU, Ubuntu 24.04 LTS, Mumbai
• Scheduling: systemd timers (no cron drift)
• Display: Xvfb virtual display (headless Chrome)
• Code: Private GitHub repo with server deploy key
• Monitoring: Self-alert via WhatsApp on any failure

NSE Holiday Aware:
The system automatically skips non-trading days using a built-in NSE holiday calendar. No manual intervention needed on market holidays.
