ABOUT PPS BITUMEN CRM

PPS Bitumen CRM is a purpose-built customer relationship management system
designed exclusively for the bitumen and road construction supply industry.

Unlike generic CRMs, PPS CRM deeply integrates with WhatsApp Business — the
primary communication channel used by contractors, dealers, and buyers in this
sector. Every incoming WhatsApp message is automatically captured, categorized,
and scored by AI, so your sales team never misses a hot lead.

The platform runs entirely on Cloudflare's global edge network, ensuring fast
response times across India and zero server maintenance overhead.

WHO IS IT FOR?
  • Bitumen traders, suppliers, and distributors
  • Sales teams managing 100s to 1000s of contacts
  • Companies that rely on WhatsApp for customer communication
  • Businesses looking to automate follow-ups and retargeting

HOW IT WORKS — STEP BY STEP

STEP 1 — CUSTOMER SENDS A WHATSAPP MESSAGE
  A contractor or dealer sends a WhatsApp message to your business number.
  Meta instantly forwards it to PPS CRM.

STEP 2 — AI READS AND SCORES THE LEAD
  Within seconds, AI analyzes the message:
  → Assigns a lead stage (HOT / WARM / COLD)
  → Detects city, product, budget, and urgency signals
  → Auto-tags the contact
  → Creates an alert for your sales team

STEP 3 — SALESPERSON RESPONDS FROM CRM
  Your team member opens the CRM, sees the alert, reads the full conversation,
  and replies — all without touching their personal phone.

STEP 4 — FOLLOW-UP IS SCHEDULED AUTOMATICALLY
  If the contact doesn't reply in X days, the system triggers an automated
  follow-up via n8n. Retargeting runs on autopilot.

STEP 5 — LEAD MOVES THROUGH THE PIPELINE
  The sales team moves the contact through the Kanban pipeline stages.
  All interactions are logged. Nothing falls through the cracks.

STEP 6 — DEAL IS CLOSED. DATA IS RECORDED.
  When the deal closes, it is logged with deal value, date, and the responsible
  salesperson. Reports and performance metrics update automatically.
