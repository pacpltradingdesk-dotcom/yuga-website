ABOUT THE SOFTWARE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

PPS Anantam's proprietary AI Dashboard is an enterprise-grade business
intelligence platform developed in-house for India's bitumen trading industry.
It combines live market data, artificial intelligence, customer relationship
management, and logistics intelligence into a single unified interface.

Designed for speed and accuracy, the platform processes data from 25+ global
and domestic APIs, runs ML-powered forecasting models, and delivers actionable
insights to our sales and operations team in real time.
